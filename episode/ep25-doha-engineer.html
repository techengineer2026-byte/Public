Episode #25

IT Jobs in Doha vs India: Salary, Tax-Free Life & .NET Career

Guest: Kamalpreet Singh

May 18, 2025 70 min Solution Architect · 20+ Yrs Recorded in Mohali Studio

About This Episode

In this episode, we sit down with Kamalpreet Singh — a seasoned Solution Architect with over 20 years of experience in the IT industry, who has worked across Mohali, Pune, and now Doha, Qatar.

Kamalpreet breaks down the real difference between IT jobs in India vs the Gulf — from tax-free salaries and cost of living in Doha, to .NET career evolution and how Punjabi professionals are building new lives in Qatar.

If you are an IT professional looking to move abroad, a .NET developer wondering about your career ceiling, or a student confused about which path to take — this episode gives you a step-by-step reality check from someone who has lived it all.

What You Will Learn in This Episode

Tax-Free Salary in Qatar vs India: Kamalpreet explains how a tax-free Gulf package compares in real take-home terms against Indian CTC packages — and why the difference is much larger than most people realise.

How to Get an IT Job in Doha: From ATS-optimised resumes to LinkedIn strategy and verified recruitment agencies — a practical step-by-step guide for Punjabi IT professionals wanting to work in the Middle East.

.NET Career in 2025: Is .NET still relevant? Kamalpreet shares 20 years of perspective on .NET evolution — from classic ASP to .NET Core — and why it remains one of the most in-demand skills in enterprise Gulf projects.

Job Scams & How to Avoid Them: Fake interviews, upfront visa fees, ghost recruiters — Kamalpreet warns about common scams targeting Indian job seekers and gives clear red flags to watch for.

Life as a Punjabi in Doha: Strict alcohol laws, driving rules, the Qatar community, and what daily life actually feels like for Punjabis living in Qatar.
