Spiritual Cleaning

The Salt Water Ritual

Maharaj insists on changing undergarments daily and soaking them in salt water to remove the 'Nazar' (evil eye) and bodily impurities that block spiritual energy.

Lost Love & Betrayal

A deep discussion on why people never forget their first love and how "Vashikaran" can be used as a remedy for those who have been cheated or left alone.

2026: The Great Cleaning

Maharaj predicts a massive global change between 2026 and 2028. He claims it is not destruction, but a "cleaning" of the earth's population and sins.

Spiritual Life Hacks (Totkay)

Small shifts in life to change your destiny

For Insomnia (Sleeplessness)

Keep a bath soap under your pillow while you sleep. On an 'Amavasya' (New Moon), throw that soap into a river or canal. It helps with deep sleep, money flow, and mental peace.

For Shani (Kumbh Rashi)

If your Saturn is heavy, massage a buffalo (jhota) with 1.25kg mustard oil on a Saturday. Read Shani Chalisa in front of it. Your bad times will vanish as the animal leaves your sight.

Protecting the Youth: The "Virgin Killer" Reality

Maharaj speaks candidly about the exploitation of young boys and girls in the modern world.

He describes the "Virgin Killer" mentality—where people manipulate pure souls only to discard them. He urges parents to stop treating schools as daycares and start being guardians. "Mind your children, or the world will corrupt them before you notice," he warns.

Key Advice: A home's protection (Nishkilan) is necessary to ensure negative energies and bad intentions don't cross your threshold.

Facing a Personal Crisis?

Whether it is a broken home, financial dispute, or a health issue that won't go away—there is a ritual for it. Don't suffer in silence when ancient wisdom is available.
