EPISODE #15

"From Student to Professional Developer: Jeet Coder’s Guide to IT Careers & Skills"

Guest: Jobanjit Singh (Jeet Coder)

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Jobanjit Singh, popularly known as Jeet Coder. Jobanjit shares his journey from a B.Tech student during the COVID-19 pandemic to becoming a prominent voice for programming in Punjabi. He dives deep into what it takes to survive in the fast-paced IT industry, why your LinkedIn profile is your digital resume, and how to stay relevant in the age of Artificial Intelligence.
