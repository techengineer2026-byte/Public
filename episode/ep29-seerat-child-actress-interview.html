EPISODE #29

Seerat: The Viral Child Actress on Acting, School, Scams & Roasting the Host

Guest: Seerat

Published: March 10, 2026  ·  42 min  ·  Episode 29

About This Episode

In this episode, we talk with Seerat who exposes the reality of being a kid in the entertainment industry, discussing how she manages to score well in exams while shooting for movies like Punjab Files and Hindi serials. She shares heartwarming details about her biggest supporters—her Nani and Mother—and hilarious, unfiltered opinions on relatives and food. Seerat also shares a crucial warning for other kids regarding online gaming scams (Roblox) and screen time, all while keeping the host on his toes with her sharp wit and "Gen Alpha" humor.
