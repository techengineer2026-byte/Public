Ayurvedic Health Secrets for Daily Life

with Dr. Pushp Sharman
Apr 12, 2026 50 Min Health & Wellness
Listen to the Episode

In this powerful episode, we sit down with Dr. Pushp Sharman, a Gold Medalist Ayurvedic expert with 30+ years of experience (practicing since 1994). We dive deep into how modern lifestyle—especially mobile phones and bad eating habits—is destroying our health, and how ancient Ayurvedic secrets can bring us back to balance.

"Ayurveda doesn't just suppress symptoms; it constructs health from the inside out. Construction takes time, but it lasts forever."

📱 What Are People Doing Nowadays?

Dr. Sharman points out a massive shift in human behavior, especially after 2007, when smartphones took over our lives.

Becoming Phone Addicts

Scrolling through reels for hours causes rapid chemical changes in the brain. This constant dopamine chase leads to severe anxiety, depression, and sleep disorders.

Giving Kids "Digital Opium"

Parents hand kids phones just to keep them quiet. This damages their mental growth, focus, and social skills. When the phone is taken away, kids react like drug addicts going through withdrawal.

Overeating & Zero Movement

Ancient Ayurveda recommended eating twice a day. Today, people eat 5+ times a day—chai, snacks, heavy meals. Combined with zero physical activity, it leads to fatty livers and obesity.

Suppressing Symptoms

Instead of curing diseases (Acidity, BP, Sugar, Thyroid), modern medicine just suppresses the symptoms. Patients end up taking pills for life without ever truly healing the root cause.

💡 The Golden Ayurvedic Rule

"Construction takes time (building health), but destruction is instant. Ayurveda builds your health from the inside out, so be patient with the process!"

🌿 Top Ayurvedic Secrets & Remedies

1. Supercharge Kids' Brain Power

Give children a 1/4th teaspoon of Brahmi with ghee or milk daily. It drastically improves memory, focus, and mental capacity—even in perfectly healthy kids. It acts like a miracle for brain development.

2. Fixing Allergies & Acidity Naturally

Stop taking daily pills and remove these 4 things from your diet:

  • Sugar & Sweets
  • Sour things (Dahi/Yogurt, pickles, chutneys)
  • Packaged foods (Noodles, pizza, bottled juices)
  • Artificial colors & preservatives

Remedy: Take 1 spoon of Amla powder with water on an empty stomach. Add Harad if constipated.

3. Cervical Pain & Stress Relief

Your neck holds up a 5-6 kg head. When you are stressed, neck muscles tighten, causing cervical issues. Remedy: Practice Anulom Vilom (5 mins morning/evening) to balance the brain's hemispheres and relax the neck naturally.

4. Thyroid Management (No More Lifelong Pills!)

Thyroid issues are metabolic disorders, not life sentences. Practice Surya Namaskar, do Anulom Vilom, and take Ayurvedic medicines like Thyro-Wel or Kanchan Guggul under guidance to eventually stop your daily thyroid pill.

5. The Magic of Panchkarma

Panchkarma isn't just a massage! It is a 5-step scientific detox process (Vamana, Virechana, Vasti, Nasya, Raktamokshana) that pulls the root causes of disease out of the body so they never return. It cures by constructing health, not destroying symptoms.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can Ayurveda cure thyroid permanently?
Yes. Ayurveda treats thyroid as a metabolic disorder (Mand Agni). By correcting digestion, practicing Surya Namaskar, Anulom Vilom, and using specific herbs like Thyro-Wel, the root cause is treated, potentially eliminating the need for lifelong pills.
How to fix acidity and allergies without pills?
Stop eating sugar, sour foods (dahi, pickles), and packaged/junk foods. Take 1 spoon of Amla powder with water on an empty stomach daily. Maintain a healthy sleep schedule to boost immunity. If needed, add Harad powder for constipation.
Why is mobile phone addiction bad for kids?
Giving kids phones acts like "digital opium". It stops their mental growth, focus, and social skills. Rapid chemical changes in the brain from scrolling cause anxiety, depression, and addictive behavior. When the phone is taken away, they show severe withdrawal symptoms.
What is the golden rule of eating in Ayurveda?
Ayurveda says: Fill your stomach with 2 parts solid food, 1 part water/liquid, and leave 1 part empty for air/digestion. Also, never drink large amounts of water right before or right after a meal—just sip a little.
