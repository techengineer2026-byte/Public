Frequently Asked Questions for Guests

How do I appear on a Punjabi podcast?

The best way is to apply directly to shows you admire. For our podcast, simply fill out the application form on this page. Clearly explain your story and why it would be interesting to a Punjabi audience.

Is there a fee to be a guest?

No, there is never a fee to be a guest on Puadh Punjabi Podcast. We invite guests based on the value and authenticity of their stories. We provide the studio and production free of charge for our featured guests.

What should I prepare before the podcast interview?

Just be yourself! Our conversations are unscripted. The best preparation is to simply think about the key points of your story you'd like to share. We'll handle the rest and guide the conversation.

How long does a recording session take?

Plan for about 90 minutes at our studio in Mohali. This gives us time to set up, have a 60-75 minute relaxed conversation, and wrap up.
