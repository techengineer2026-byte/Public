Common Questions

Why do you use the Sony FX3?

The Sony FX3 is a dedicated cinema camera. Unlike standard DSLRs, it can record for hours without overheating and provides 15+ stops of dynamic range for professional color grading.

Can I bring my own gear?

Yes, our studio is "plug-and-play" ready. However, we recommend using our Shure SM7B setup for the best acoustic matching in our treated room.
