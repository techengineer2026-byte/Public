Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent the podcast studio space?

₹1,000 per hour. Half-day (4 hours) is ₹7,000 (save ₹1,000) and full-day (8 hours) is ₹11,000 (save ₹4,000). The price includes the soundproofed room, podcast desk, seating, AC, WiFi, and free parking. Lights and equipment are available on request at extra cost.

What's included in the studio space rental?

The rental includes a fully soundproofed recording room with acoustic foam, professional podcast desk with seating for 2-4 people, branded backdrop, air conditioning, WiFi, power outlets, and free parking. LED lighting, cameras, microphones, and other equipment can be added on request.

Can I bring my own equipment?

Yes! Many clients bring their own cameras, microphones, and laptops. The studio space is designed to accommodate your setup. We provide the room, desk, soundproofing, and power — you bring your gear.

Are lights included in the price?

Basic ambient lighting is included. Professional LED panel lights with adjustable color temperature are available on request at additional cost. Half-day and full-day packages include basic LED lighting.

How many people can use the studio?

The studio comfortably seats 2-4 people at the podcast desk. For larger panels or group discussions with up to 6 people, give us advance notice and we'll adjust the seating arrangement.

Can I use the studio for YouTube videos or courses?

Yes! The studio is used for podcast recording, YouTube videos, interviews, product reviews, online course filming, brand shoots, and corporate content. It's a versatile space adaptable to different setups.

What are the operating hours?

Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM. Sunday: 11:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Early morning and late evening slots are available on request. We recommend booking 48 hours in advance for weekend slots.

Where is the studio located?

Plot F-298, R&R Tower, Phase 8B, Industrial Area, Sector 74, Mohali, Punjab 160055. 10 minutes from Chandigarh, easily accessible from Kharar, Zirakpur, and Panchkula. Free parking available.

How do I book?

WhatsApp us at +91 83607 69451 with your preferred date, time, and duration. We confirm within 1 hour. Or fill out the booking form above. Book 48 hours in advance for guaranteed weekend availability.
