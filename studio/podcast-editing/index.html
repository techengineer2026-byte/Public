Frequently Asked Questions

How much does podcast editing cost?

₹5,000 per episode. This includes editing up to 1 hour of footage, 6 Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts, color grading with DaVinci Resolve, audio mixing with Adobe Audition, subtitles, and a custom YouTube thumbnail. Bundle: 4 episodes for ₹18,000. Monthly: 8 episodes for ₹32,000.

What software do you use for editing?

Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing, timeline work, multi-camera sync, and subtitle generation. DaVinci Resolve for Hollywood-grade color grading and color correction. Adobe Audition for audio cleanup, noise removal, and professional sound mixing. This three-software workflow delivers broadcast-quality results.

How many Reels or Shorts are included?

Every ₹5,000 editing package includes 6 Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts. These are vertical 9:16 clips with animated captions, hook text, and engaging transitions — designed to drive traffic back to your full episode.

How long does editing take?

Standard delivery is 48–72 hours after receiving the raw footage. Rush delivery within 24 hours is available on request. Bundle and retainer clients get priority turnaround.

Do you add subtitles and captions?

Yes. Every edit includes burnt-in subtitles on the full episode and animated captions on all 6 Reels/Shorts. Subtitles are available in Punjabi, Hindi, and English. We use AI transcription plus manual review for accuracy.

Can I send footage remotely?

Yes! You don't need to be in Mohali. Upload your raw footage via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or any cloud service. We edit remotely and deliver final files online. Many clients are based in Delhi, Mumbai, Canada, and the UK.

What is DaVinci Resolve and why does it matter?

DaVinci Resolve is the industry-standard software for color grading — used in Hollywood films and Netflix productions. We use it to give your podcast a cinematic color profile with consistent skin tones, vibrant backgrounds, and a professional visual quality that makes your content stand out from amateur podcasts.

Do I get a YouTube thumbnail?

Yes. Every editing package includes a custom-designed YouTube thumbnail optimized for click-through rate. It includes the guest photo, bold text, and branded elements designed to stand out in YouTube search results and recommended feeds.
